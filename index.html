👨‍👩‍👧 About Us

What happens when a tech nerd and a biotech student get curious about mushrooms? A mini mushroom farm, of course! 🧑‍🔬👩‍💻 We started this as a fun little side project — and it quickly grew roots. Now, the whole family’s in on it, helping out wherever needed — from farm setup to delivery runs. We grow clean, chemical-free mushrooms right here in Tripunithura using modern automation and a whole lot of care. And yes, they’re harvested fresh and out the door the same day!

🍄 What We Grow

Right now, we grow premium white oyster mushrooms — fresh, tender, and harvested the very same day you get them. 🍄 Grown indoors in a clean, climate-controlled setup with minimal human contact, our mushrooms are as fresh as it gets. We're also working on bringing in exotic mushroom varieties, microgreens, and sprouts soon — all grown with the same care and zero chemicals.
